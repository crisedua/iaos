# SolIA.pro — Product & Offer

> SolIA.pro is the productized “Voice Agent OS” inside AI OS for Business. This file defines what SolIA is, who it serves, and how we sell/deliver it.

---

## One-Liner

**SolIA.pro is a 24/7 AI voice agent for your phone number and website that answers questions, qualifies prospects, and captures leads automatically.**

---

## What It Does

SolIA acts like a always-on receptionist + sales assistant:

- Answers inbound calls **24/7**
- Handles FAQs about your business (services, pricing ranges, hours, location, policies)
- Asks qualifying questions (intent, urgency, budget, location, availability)
- Captures lead data (name, phone, email, reason for calling)
- Routes next steps:
  - Sends the lead to you/your team (WhatsApp/email/CRM/Google Sheet)
  - Can hand off to a human when needed
  - Can prompt booking intent (optionally via link or request for callback)

## How It Works (High-Level)

1. We ingest your business info (website, docs, FAQs, offers)
2. We set the voice, tone, and the call flow (what to ask, what to capture)
3. We connect SolIA to:
   - A phone number (existing or new)
   - A website voice widget (optional)
4. We configure lead capture + routing to your preferred destination
5. We test with real scenarios, tune, then go live

## Boundaries / Guardrails

- The agent should never invent policies or prices
- When uncertain, it should ask clarifying questions or offer a callback
- It should clearly capture contact info before ending a call
- It should follow a polite, professional tone aligned to the brand
