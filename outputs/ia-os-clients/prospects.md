## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `Identified` | You know they exist and fit the ICP — haven't contacted yet |
| `Contacted` | First message sent — no reply yet |
| `Responded` | They replied — conversation is open |
| `Diagnostic Scheduled` | 30-min diagnostic call is on the calendar |
| `Diagnostic Done` | Call happened — next step is proposal or follow-up |
| `Proposal Sent` | You sent the IA OS package proposal |
| `Closed Won` | They said yes and paid or committed |
| `Closed Lost` | They said no or went silent after 3 follow-ups |
