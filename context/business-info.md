## Business Overview

**Business Name:** AI OS for Business  
**What we do:** We help businesses install an “AI Operating System” — a practical set of AI agents, automations, workflows, and playbooks that produce measurable results.  
**Type:** AI consulting + productized implementation packages + training  
**Region:** Chile / LatAm (delivery possible in Spanish and English)

AI OS for Business is focused on real operations: reducing manual work, responding faster to leads/customers, and turning repetitive processes into reliable AI-assisted systems.

## Who We Serve

**Primary customers**
- Local service businesses (real estate, dentists, clinics, professional services)
- Small business owners who want ROI without hiring a tech team
- Teams with repetitive customer inquiries, lead handling, and admin work

**Ideal buyer**
- Owner/manager who feels: “we’re losing leads, wasting time, and moving too slow”
- Wants speed, clarity, and a done-for-you implementation

## Core Offers

### 1) AI OS Setup (Core Product)
A packaged implementation that installs the foundations:
- Lead capture + qualification flow
- Knowledge base (from website/docs) for consistent answers
- Automations (handoffs, alerts, data capture, follow-ups)
- Basic analytics + iteration plan

**Outcome examples**
- Faster responses
- Fewer missed leads
- Reduced repetitive admin work
- Clearer operational visibility

---

### 2) Voice Agent OS (SolIA) — 24/7 Lead Handling
AI voice agent that:
- Answers calls 24/7 (and/or website voice widget when relevant)
- Handles FAQs, captures contact data, qualifies leads
- Routes next steps (handoff to team, booking link, CRM/Sheet)

**Delivery includes**
- Script + tone setup
- Business knowledge base ingestion
- Testing with real scenarios
- Go-live + tuning loop

**Upsells**
- WhatsApp voice agent
- Integrations (CRM, calendars, support tools)
- Advanced reporting / dashboards

---

### 3) Training OS (EduIA) — Enablement & Adoption
Practical training and playbooks so teams actually use the system:
- “AI for daily operations” workflows
- Prompting templates + SOPs
- Mini-courses / workshops for non-technical teams

---

### 4) Magia.my — AI Workspace (Productivity Layer)
A multi-model AI workspace for non-technical users to run workflows, reuse prompts, and standardize outputs across the business.

## Operating Model

**Acquisition channels**
- Google Maps prospecting → cold call / WhatsApp (primary — find local businesses, call direct)
- WhatsApp communities and groups (secondary)
- Referrals + partnerships (web agencies, marketers, accountants)
- Limited-slot pilots / trial offers (time-boxed)

**Delivery flow (standard)**
1. Discovery (pain, current process, constraints, success metrics)
2. Install v1 (agent/workflow + knowledge base + data capture)
3. Test & tune (edge cases, prompts, routing, reliability)
4. Go-live + monitoring
5. Expand (WhatsApp, integrations, dashboards, more workflows)
